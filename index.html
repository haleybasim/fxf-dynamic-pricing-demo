Where dynamic pricing delivers

Four scenarios where capacity-based pricing creates real value for FedEx Freight.

📈
Peak demand capture
When a lane is running at 85%+ utilization, standard pricing leaves margin on the table. Dynamic pricing lets you charge what the market supports.
+8 to +15% revenue uplift
🛍
Backhaul incentives
Return legs with empty capacity sit at 30-45% utilization. A price reduction of 10-18% fills the truck and converts deadhead miles into revenue.
+10 to +18% volume on backhauls
🗒
Seasonal surge management
Q4 retail replenishment, produce seasons, and holiday shipping create predictable demand spikes. Surge pricing protects capacity for premium shippers.
Revenue protection during peak
⚡
Spot market competition
Shippers comparing quotes in real time respond to price signals. Dynamic rates let FedEx Freight win incremental spot freight without eroding contract pricing.
Compete on low-demand lanes
